Introduction to Apps about Statistical Legislation

The apps on this page help you with basic understanding of statistical legislation and help you with changes in your existing legislation.

Please find links to the apps below:

  • C.3.1.2 Understand the Generic Statistical Law
  • C.3.3.1 Evaluate an existing law
  • C.3.3.3 Improve an existing law

General Information for All Apps

Language and starter conversations

  1. Choose your to have the dialogue in your own language. Just write “From now on use YOUR LANGUAGE” e.g. Arabic
  2. Choose starter conversations. e.g. give me an overview of chapters.

Intial clarification Questions and background information

  1. Insert you message and the app will ask clarification questions checking terms and understanding of your question. e.g., you cannot ask for dog food in this context

  2. Get background information (e.g., details about scope of statistical law in the generic law on official statistics.)

You can suppress initial qustions & background information by writing “Do not include initial questions and background information”. See example in starter below.

Understand the Generic Statistical Law

This app helps you understand the Generic Statistical Law through quizzes and Q&A, providing references to the law itself as well as to the UN Quality Framework.

Evaluate your Existing Law

TThis app will evaluate your existing law (or a law from another country) using the generic law and UN-NQAF quality assurance framework. It will provide you with information about compliance and suggest changes in order to comply with the generic law.

Note on workflow: Use the C.3.3.2 Improve existing law app if you want suggested changes related to a specific issue. After improving the law you can evaluate the improved law by using the C.3.3.1 Evaluate existing law.

Resources

  • Download the Generic Statistical Law document
  • Download the UN-NQAF Principles and Requirements document
  • For testing purposes you can download the Statistical Act for Denmark to your local drive and upload this when you run the app. Download Act on Statistics Denmark 2018

Improve your Existing Law

This app will help you to improve your existing law focusing on a specific issue. E.g. coordination of data collection from admin sources. Steps: 1. present problem/issue; 2. Upload law; and 3 evaluate.

Note on workflow: Use the C.3.3.2 Improve existing law app if you want suggested changes related to a specific issue. After improving the law you can evaluate the improved law by using the C.3.3.1 Evaluate existing law.

Resources

  • For testing purposes you can download the Statistical Act for Denmark to your local drive and upload this when you run the app. Download Act on Statistics Denmark 2018
  • You can also download a Generic Model Statistical Law if you want to start from scratch. The model statistical law must be supplemented with names and relevant changes. Download Generic Model Statistical Law
