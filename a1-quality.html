Introduction to Apps about Quality in Statistics

The apps on this page help you with basic understanding of Quality, helps you with how to conduct, understand and analyse self-assessment and last but not least how to solve problems using the UN-NQAF quality assurance framework.

Please find links to the apps below:

  • A.1.1.1 Understand the UN National Quality Assurance Framework (UN-NQAF)
  • A.4.3.2 Initial problem analysis UN National Quality Assurance Framework (UN-NQAF)

General Information for All Apps

Language and starter conversations

  1. Choose your to have the dialogue in your own language. Just write “From now on use YOUR LANGUAGE” e.g. Arabic
  2. Choose starter conversations. e.g. give me an overview of chapters.

Intial clarification Questions and background information

  1. Insert you message and the app will ask clarification questions checking terms and understanding of your question. e.g., you cannot ask for dog food in this context

  2. Get background information (e.g., details about scope of statistical law in the generic law on official statistics.)

You can suppress initial qustions & background information by writing “Do not include initial questions and background information”. See example in starter below.

A.4.3.2 Initial problem analysis UN National Quality Assurance Framework (UN-NQAF)

This app helps provides you with an initial problem analysis based on your problem or issues. It based on these three steps:

  1. You input text about problem and the app helps you with terminology and clarifications
  2. The app suggest change options using the quality framework and other knowledge documents
  3. You evalute and choose a suitable solution and the app prepares an initial problem analysis to you
Note: this app only provides an initial problem analysis without detailed planning of capability developement projects. See cross-cutting section.'
